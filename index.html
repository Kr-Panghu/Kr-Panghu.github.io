Bootstrapping Diffusion Models: Iterative Synthetic Data Generation for Self-Supervised Learning
Kangrui Cen, Yuxiao Yang, Shuze Chen, Ziqi Huang, Tianyu Zhang
CS3964: Image Processing and Computer Vision, 2023 Fall

Summary: We introduce a novel bootstrapping approach for training generative models. Specifically, we construct synthetic datasets by combining generated samples from previous iterations with real data. By recycling samples over successive generations, this technique reduces the dependence on large curated datasets while producing varied outputs.
